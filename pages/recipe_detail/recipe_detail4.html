Grilled Chicken



Back to All Recipes

Overview

CategoryMain Course
CuisineItalian
Meal TypeDinner
DifficultyEasy
Prep Time10 mins
Cook Time25 mins
Servings2
Dietary InfoGluten Free, Dairy Free

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Rosemary
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Mix olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, and rosemary in a bowl.
  2. Coat the chicken with the marinade and leave for 10 minutes.
  3. Heat a grill pan over high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for 12 mins each side until cooked through.
  5. Rest for 5 minutes before serving.
