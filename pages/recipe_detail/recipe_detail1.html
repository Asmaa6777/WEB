CategoryMain Course
CuisineItalian
Meal TypeDinner
DifficultyEasy
Prep Time10 mins
Cook Time20 mins
Servings4
Dietary InfoDairy Free

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Milk

Instructions

  1. Boil salted water and cook pasta until al dente.
  2. Brown the beef in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add chopped onion and garlic, cook until soft.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and a splash of milk. Simmer for 10 mins.
  5. Mix in the cooked pasta and serve hot.
