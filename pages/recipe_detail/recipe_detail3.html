Chocolate Cake



Back to All Recipes

Overview

CategoryDessert
CuisineFrench
Meal TypeSnack
DifficultyMedium
Prep Time20 mins
Cook Time35 mins
Servings8
Dietary InfoVegetarian

Ingredients

  • Dark Chocolate
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Melt dark chocolate with oil in a bowl.
  3. Whisk eggs and sugar together until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour, then the chocolate mixture and milk.
  5. Pour into a greased tin and bake for 35 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving.
