Pancake



Back to All Recipes

Overview

CategoryDessert
CuisineFrench
Meal TypeBreakfast
DifficultyEasy
Prep Time10 mins
Cook Time15 mins
Servings4
Dietary InfoVegetarian

Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Sugar
  • Flour

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs and sugar together in a bowl.
  2. Add flour and mix until smooth.
  3. Stir in vanilla and heavy cream.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour a ladle of batter and cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  6. Serve with your favourite toppings.
