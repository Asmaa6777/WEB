Vegetable Soup



Back to All Recipes

Overview

CategoryAppetizer
CuisineFrench
Meal TypeLunch
DifficultyEasy
Prep Time15 mins
Cook Time30 mins
Servings4
Dietary InfoVegan, Gluten Free, Dairy Free

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Potatoes
  • Tomatoes
  • Green Beans

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  3. Add chopped carrots, potatoes, and green beans.
  4. Pour in enough water to cover the vegetables.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Simmer for 30 minutes until vegetables are tender.
