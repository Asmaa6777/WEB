Mahshy



Back to All Recipes

Overview

CategoryMain Course
CuisineArabic
Meal TypeLunch
DifficultyHard
Prep Time40 mins
Cook Time60 mins
Servings6
Dietary InfoVegan, Gluten Free

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Fresh Herbs
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onions
  • Spices

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Mix rice with fresh herbs, onions, and spices.
  3. Stuff the mixture into vegetables of your choice.
  4. Arrange stuffed vegetables in a pot and pour tomato sauce over them.
  5. Cook on low heat for 60 minutes until fully cooked.
